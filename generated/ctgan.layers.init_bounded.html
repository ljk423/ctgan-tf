ctgan.layers.init_bounded¶

ctgan.layers.init_bounded(shape, **kwargs)[source]¶

Initializes the weights or bias of a fully connected layer according to the dim passed in kwargs. They are computed according to \(\mathcal{U}(-\sqrt{k}, \sqrt{k})\) where \(k=\frac{1}{\text{dim}}\).

Parameters
shape: tf.Shape

Shape of the tensor to initialize.

kwargs: dict

Dictionary of parameters, containing dim and dtype.

Returns
tf.Tensor

Tensor with the initialiazed weights of shape shape, sampled from \(\mathcal{U}(-\sqrt{k}, \sqrt{k})\) where \(k=\frac{1}{\text{dim}}\).
